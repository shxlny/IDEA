FAQ

1. What is our platform?
Answer: Our platform is a space for sharing ideas, where users can share their concepts, get feedback, and develop their projects in a community of like-minded people.
2. How can I submit my idea?
Answer: You can submit your idea by filling out the form on our website. Just go to the "Share an idea" section and follow the instructions.
3. How are ideas evaluated?
Answer: Ideas are rated by other users on the platform. You can get feedback and ratings that will help you improve your concept.
4. Can I comment on other users' ideas?
Answer: Yes! We encourage discussion and constructive criticism. You can leave comments and feedback on other users' ideas.
5. How can I contact the support team?
Answer: To contact our support team, you can write to us at the email address listed in the "Contact" section.
6. Is there a limit to the number of ideas I can submit?
Answer: No, you can submit as many ideas as you like. We love to see diversity and creativity!
7. How can I track the status of my idea?
Answer: You can track the status of your idea in your personal account on our website. There you will see all the reviews and ratings.
8. What are the rules for using the platform?
Answer: All users must follow the rules of the community, including respect for other participants and respect for copyright. The full list of rules is available in the "Rules" section.
